AAC Jet!

A simple communication board app built for everyday use, including older Android devices and small screens.

  • Made for real family life, where children can easily drop or damage devices.
  • Designed to run on older hardware, even devices manufactured in 2011.
  • No setup required: organise images into folders and subfolders, copy them onto the device like a flash drive, and the app builds the scrollable communication board automatically.
  • Pinch-to-zoom resizes cards easily, with support for landscape and portrait modes on phones, tablets, and low-resolution screens.
  • Includes keyboard input for fast communication.
